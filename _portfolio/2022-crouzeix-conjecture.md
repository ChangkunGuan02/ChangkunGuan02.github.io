Crouzeix's conjecture is a central open problem in matrix analysis, bounding the norm of a matrix function by twice its supremum on the numerical range. This project studied the **level set Crouzeix conjecture** — a weak version that applies to finite compressions of the shift.

**Key contributions:**
- Established the level set Crouzeix conjecture for several classes of 3&times;3, 4&times;4, and 5&times;5 nilpotent matrices via a geometric analysis of their numerical ranges
- Developed a Mathematica-based tool for fast visualization of numerical ranges
- Presented at the Joint Mathematics Meetings 2023 (Boston); published in *Concrete Operators* (2024)

**Principal Investigator:** Dr. Kelly Bickel
