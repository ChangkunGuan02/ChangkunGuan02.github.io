My honors thesis introduced and studied the **Precedence-Constrained Quadratic Knapsack Problem (PC-QKP)**, a previously unstudied NP-hard nonlinear combinatorial optimization problem that combines the quadratic knapsack objective with precedence constraints on item selection.

**Key contributions:**
- Developed an exact solution method for the PC-QKP
- Designed and experimented with heuristics and metaheuristics
- Generated difficult problem instances to probe what makes the problem hard, informing solution strategies for this and similar problems

**Thesis advisor:** Dr. Lucas Waddell
