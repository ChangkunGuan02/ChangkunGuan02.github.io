This paper studies the level set Crouzeix conjecture, a weak version of Crouzeix's conjecture that applies to finite compressions of the shift. Among other results, it establishes the level set Crouzeix conjecture for several classes of 3&times;3, 4&times;4, and 5&times;5 matrices associated to compressions of the shift, via a geometric analysis of their numerical ranges.
