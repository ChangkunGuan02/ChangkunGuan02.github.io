Order fulfillment is a fundamental challenge in large-scale e-commerce, requiring real-time decisions for every incoming order. This work introduces a two-stage contextual stochastic optimization framework that captures delivery timeliness and future inventory consumption uncertainty, and develops deep neural networks to approximate its solutions for real-time deployment during peak hours, when traditional solvers are computationally prohibitive.
