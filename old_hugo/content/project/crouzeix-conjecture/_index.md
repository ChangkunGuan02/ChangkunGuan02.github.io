Presented research findings at the Joint Mathematics Meetings 2023 at Boston as a speaker. Accepted to Concrete Operator. Formulated an analytical proof for Crouzeix's conjecture in the context of nilpotent matrices and extended the numerical result to higher-dimensional cases. Developed a Mathematica-based tool for quick visualisation of numerical ranges, enhancing data interpretation efficiency.

**Principal Investigator:** Dr. Kelly Bickel

**Key Contributions:**
- Formulated analytical proof for Crouzeix's conjecture for nilpotent matrices
- Extended numerical results to higher-dimensional cases
- Developed Mathematica visualization tool for numerical ranges
- Presented at Joint Mathematics Meetings 2023
- Published in Concrete Operator
